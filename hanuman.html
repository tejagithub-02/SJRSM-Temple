Hanuman Jayanti (May 5th)

Hanuman Jayanti at Sri Jayarama Seva Mandali is a vibrant celebration of Lord Hanuman’s birth, marked by pujas, chanting, and community activities near the temple in Bengaluru.

What is Hanuman Jayanti?
Hanuman Jayanti commemorates the birth of Lord Hanuman, the devoted follower of Lord Rama, known for his strength and loyalty. Celebrated on Chaitra Purnima (typically April/May), it features rituals like Hanuman Chalisa recitations, Abhisheka, and community gatherings.

[](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hanuman_Jayanti)

Activities Near the Temple:
On Hanuman Jayanti (e.g., April 12, 2025), the area around the Mandali buzzes with devotion:
- Morning pujas and Abhisheka starting at 5:30 AM.
- Hanuman Chalisa and Sundar Kanda recitations.
- Evening processions with colorful lights and bhajans.
- Prasad distribution (e.g., ladoos) and community service.

[](https://theartarium.com/blogs/news/hanuman-jayanti-2025?srsltid=AfmBOope54-apPBeiLGDbJ3XTjI4Ds6eFr_coBm6CJmOM46PX8XVxFof)[](https://www.agoda.com/travel-guides/india/chennai/explore-nanganallur-anjaneyar-temple-hanuman-jayanti-fun/)[](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/nagpur/hanuman-jayanti-celebrations-processions-rituals-and-devotion-fill-the-city/articleshow/120241560.cms)

Spiritual Significance:
The festival inspires devotees to emulate Hanuman’s devotion and courage, strengthening spiritual bonds through shared worship and service.

[](https://pujayagna.com/blogs/hindu-festivals/hanuman-jayanti?srsltid=AfmBOoq3d-JTJ_R9K1210G50JcBlMpd7Ahji8SmOZxDPuYUjZAWyvwm8)

How to Participate:
Join in-person for pujas or watch live streams on our YouTube channel. Volunteers can assist with seva like decorating the Hanuman shrine or distributing prasad.
